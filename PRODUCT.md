# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

High-end homeowners seeking luxury finishes, and interior designers/architects looking for premium application partners for residential and commercial landmarks.

## Product Purpose

Exhibiting premium coating craftsmanship, texture patterns, transformations, and signature finishes to establish trust and generate qualified leads.

## Brand Personality

Sophisticated, Craftsmanship, Meticulous. Refined dark theme with warm accents, detailed texture views, and professional presentation.

## Anti-references

Generic corporate template styles, flat SaaS landing pages, loud/neon colors, or cheap layout templates that don't reflect high-end luxury services.

## Design Principles

- **Texture First**: Display premium finishes in full high-fidelity detail so the craftsmanship is immediately clear.
- **Warm & Sophisticated**: Use a cohesive, deep, warm-toned palette that evokes exclusivity and premium quality.
- **Clean Simplicity**: Ensure navigation and content are straightforward, letting the work speak for itself without unnecessary layouts or widgets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain high text contrast (>4.5:1), support reduced motion requests for entry animations, and ensure keyboard navigability for form controls.
